About

SoftNotes is webiste that has most of the esstianials that students like you need all in one place. Check out all the tools we have for you. Our tools are divided into three categories: Math, Science and English. Math contains relevant mathematical formulas that students need along with a calculator to perform quick calcualtions. English has guides on how to do proper citations along with links to useful sites do research if need be. Science also has relevant scientific formulas with a calculator.
